Smart Manufuacturing

It is a combination of human creativity, digitally connected machines and assets, and AI-powered systems and analytics. The integration of AI and smart tools helps fuel adaptability and speeds up the capacity to customise outputs based upon real-time data and intel.

Smart manufacturing integrates advanced technologies such as AI, machine learning, and data analytics to create a connected and intelligent production ecosystem. Artificial Intelligence is employed to enable machines and systems to learn from data, make decisions, and adapt to changing conditions in real-time. Machine learning algorithms analyze vast datasets generated by sensors and devices to identify patterns, predict outcomes, and optimize processes. Data analytics plays a pivotal role by extracting actionable insights from the plethora of information collected throughout the manufacturing lifecycle. Through seamless integration, these technologies work cohesively, empowering manufacturers with the ability to achieve predictive maintenance, enhance product quality, optimize resource utilization, and make informed, data-driven decisions that contribute to overall operational excellence.

BVM Smart Manufuacturing internship

Under the Samarth Udyog Mission of Ministry of Heavy Industry (MHI), Govt. of India, IIT Delhi and Automation Industry Association (AIA) together with Industry sponsors have set up facilities, IITD-AIA Foundation for Smart Manufacturing (FSM) that help, support, and develop Smart Manufacturing concepts for Indian Industry to witness, ideate and try out in their own plants. The collaboration is also aimed at developing a holistic educational curriculum and skill building program through a vibrant incubation and administrative environment. FSM Skills is a holistic platform for providing immersive training experience through Live lectures, Online learning, Live demonstrations, Live Labs and Self paced exercises on remotely accessible actual hardware.

Machine Learing Project

  • FSMbot

    • The project centres on creating a specialized chatbot for smart manufacturing technology, with the goal of enhancing user experience and knowledge acquisition within the industry. This chatbot will cater to product inquiries and offer interactive information retrieval, aligning closely with the objectives of advancing smart manufacturing technologies.

    Our smart manufacturing chatbot is a cutting-edge conversational interface designed to assist users in navigating and understanding the complexities of modern manufacturing processes. Leveraging AI and natural language processing, the chatbot provides real-time responses to queries, guiding users through troubleshooting procedures, and offering insights into production workflows. It is equipped with machine learning capabilities, learning from user interactions to continuously improve its responses and adapt to evolving manufacturing scenarios. The chatbot serves as a valuable tool for quick problem resolution, providing information on equipment status, process optimizations, and even facilitating communication between different components of the manufacturing system. Ultimately, our smart manufacturing chatbot enhances user experience, streamlines communication, and contributes to the efficiency and intelligence of the overall manufacturing environment.
